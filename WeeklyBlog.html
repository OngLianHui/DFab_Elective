    3D Printing

    I did a 3d design for a horse chess piece using fusion 360

    The process for this was using Fusion360 and design a chesspiece with reference to an image i sourced from online.

    I then proceeded to trace out the image using the Line function.

    The challenging part of this process was when i had to revolve the traced out line. As it was relative to the origin of the

    default origin. I had difficulty trying to revolve the tracing as the tracing was not on the default origin, or anywhere close

    I then figured out how it worked and shifted my tracing onto the default origin, making the base of the tracing as centered

    as possible.
